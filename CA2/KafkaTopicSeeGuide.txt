1. List all topics

bin/kafka-topics.sh \
  --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 \
  --list

This will print every topic name on your broker.



2. Describe a topic (partitions, replicas, offsets)

bin/kafka-topics.sh \
  --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 \
  --describe \
  --topic <your_topic_name>

That shows you partition counts, leader/ISR, and log size (i.e. highâ€‘watermark offsets).



3. Peek at the messages in a topic

bin/kafka-console-consumer.sh \
  --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 \
  --topic <your_topic_name> \
  --from-beginning \
  --max-messages 10
